welcome to pig-dice.

It is during the fall of world war 1 and the SSR and the Allies are on the verge of achieving world peace. The US has just dropped a bomb at Hiroshima. This stains their identity in the war from their classy brand of ‘men of honor’ to ‘blood thirsty hounds’.Every army is currently terrified by their very existence, apart from the Russians. Of course, it had to be the Russians! Those a**holes!

A young American corpral tries to drive his partially scared infantry force through a small town of Cicily Russia, and whoaaa!!!! They come face to face with the ruthless and merciless Russians. There tends to be a standoff, as the two are afraid of the possible mutual alienation. Any uncalculated steps could lead to a blood-bath. Everyone takes position and wait for their fate. Extreme silence and nerve wrecking tension rents the air, apart from the frequent croaking of motor components, and the frequent screams of the birds of prey flying in tight circles above the field. They seemed like they were cheering! The tension is so intense that one feels like you can hear the insurgents heartbeat better than your own.

The young American corpral takes a white blood-stained cloth, ties it to the tip of his gun, a symbol of peace, and walks into the smoky field separating the two forces. The Russian commander first preps his team for a possible dog-fight, then steps forward and meets up with the American corpral, and a sketchy truce is reached.

They decide to play a game that would spare lives, and let the winner keep the territory. They settle for PIGDICE! PIGDICE.. what the f*ck! I wish they settled for something interesting.. like the Russian roulette or something! This isn’t charity! it’s a WAR! Anyway, Pigdice, alright.. lets see who’ll win.
